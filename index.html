Why Mediterranean Cooking Matters

Italian and Spanish cuisines share the same Mediterranean philosophy - respect for ingredients, seasonal cooking, simple preparations that let quality shine - but each has unique techniques and traditions.

Understanding both gives you the complete picture of Mediterranean cooking. You'll see how similar ingredients are treated differently, how techniques overlap and diverge, and how to bring the best of both worlds into your own kitchen.
